Precision Piercing & Fine Jewellery


At Oldstone Tattoo House, Dylan delivers expert piercings with the highest quality ASTM-F136 titanium jewellery and Class B Enbio sterilisation — safe, professional, and made to last.

Important Information

  • Earlobe piercings — Available from age 8+, if the child requests it and it is safe to perform, with a parent present.
  • Cartilage piercings — 14+, with a parent present to sign the consent form.
  • Facial piercings — 16+, with a parent present to sign the consent form.
  • Navel (belly button) piercings — 16+, with a parent present to sign the consent form.
  • Nipple and tongue piercings — Strictly 18+, with valid physical photo ID required.
